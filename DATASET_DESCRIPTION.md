# Cancer ICU Prediction Dataset

## Overview
Clinical and demographic data of cancer patients admitted to the ICU for mortality and 30-day survival prediction.

## Source
The dataset was derived from the ambispective EVA (Evidence–Verification–Analysis) cohort, conducted at a high-complexity comprehensive oncology hospital in Bogotá, Colombia, serving as a national referral center for patients with solid and hematologic malignancies requiring specialized critical care.

## Features
- **Demographic**: Age, sex, body mass index
- **Oncologic**: Primary tumor site, cancer status, performance status, driver mutations, recent treatments
- **Physiological & Laboratory**: Hematologic, renal, metabolic, and coagulation markers (±6 hours of ICU admission)
- **Therapeutic Interventions**: Vasopressors, methylene blue, renal replacement therapy, mechanical ventilation
- **Clinical Context**: Admission type, ICU readmission, infection/shock presence, CAM-ICU delirium assessment, SAPS III, APACHE II, SOFA scores

## Usage
Research on clinical risk prediction models in critically ill oncology patients.

## Ethics
Data is de-identified and complies with HIPAA and IRB guidelines. Approved by the local Research and Ethics Committee (Cayre Approval No. 164 of 2025) with informed consent obtained from participants or legal representatives.
